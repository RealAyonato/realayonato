Data Storage

Your order data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore — a trusted, enterprise-grade cloud database. Firebase is fully compliant with GDPR and international data protection standards.

Data is retained only for as long as needed to complete and manage your order, after which it can be deleted upon request.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage solely for two lightweight purposes:

✅ No tracking cookies. We don't monitor your browsing or build profiles about you.
